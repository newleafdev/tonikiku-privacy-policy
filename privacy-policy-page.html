Tonikiku Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 05, 2025

Welcome to Tonikiku ("we," "us," or "our"). We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application ("App"). Our App is designed to operate without collecting any personal data from you.

1. Information We Collect

No Data Collection by Us: Our App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data about you. All data, such as files you create or save, remains on your device and is not accessed or processed by us.

Apple's Data Collection: When you use the App on an iOS device, Apple may collect certain information automatically, such as device type, iOS version, or crash logs, as part of its system diagnostics or analytics. This is controlled by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy (available at apple.com/privacy). We do not have access to this data.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any data, we do not use or share any personal information. Any files you save are stored locally on your device and remain under your control.

3. Data Security

As we do not collect or store your data, there is no risk of data breaches from our end. Files saved locally are protected by your device's security features.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children, as we collect no data at all.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy if our practices or legal requirements change. Any updates will be posted here, and your continued use of the App signifies acceptance of those changes.
